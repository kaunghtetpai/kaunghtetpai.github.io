Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how you use our website
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Provide personalized content
  • Remember your cookie consent preferences

Types of Cookies We Use

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Font Services: For loading web fonts
  • CDN Services: For faster content delivery

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Get notified when cookies are set

Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not work properly:

  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some interactive features may not function
  • We may not be able to provide personalized content
  • Website performance may be affected

Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please check this page periodically for updates.
